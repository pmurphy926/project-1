About Me

Hello!
Let me introduce myself.

I am a former educator who is looking to learn new skills that can help both teachers and students in day to day life. I know the struggles firsthand and feel as though the impact I sought to make in the lives of my students can be better address from a systemic standpoint.

I was born, grew up, and still live in Brooklyn, New York. From my days cleaning movie theaters, to my time managing a shipping department, all the way through my years of teaching I have always sought to work with my colleagues solve problems and make the work environment more welcoming and equitable.

I hope to bring my perspective as an educator, parent, and team member to my software development to build solutions to real problems.
